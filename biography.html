Biography

Olusegun Michael Otunuga is an Assistant Professor of Mathematics at Augusta University with a courtesy appointment in the School of Public Health, Department of Biostatistics, Data Science, and Epidemiology. His research focuses on stochastic modeling, mathematical epidemiology, biostatistics, and dynamic systems. His work integrates mathematical theory with applications in epidemiology, biological systems, financial modeling, and stochastic processes.

Education

  • Ph.D., Pure & Applied Mathematics University of South Florida, Tampa, FL (2014)
  • M.S., Mathematics Marshall University, Huntington, WV (2009)
  • B.Sc., Mathematics Federal University of Agriculture, Nigeria (2006)

Academic Appointments

  • Assistant Professor (Courtesy Appointment), School of Public Health – Department of Biostatistics, Data Science, and Epidemiology, Augusta University (2023–Present)
  • Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematics, Augusta University (2021–Present)
  • Assistant Professor, Department of Mathematics, Marshall University (2014–2021)
  • Graduate Teaching Assistant, Department of Mathematics & Statistics, University of South Florida (2009–2014)
  • Graduate Teaching Assistant, Department of Mathematics, Marshall University (2007–2009)

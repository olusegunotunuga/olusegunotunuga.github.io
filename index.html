Welcome

I am a faculty member in the Department of Mathematics at Augusta University. My research focuses on stochastic modeling, mathematical biology, epidemiology, and mathematical oncology.

My work studies stochastic threshold phenomena in epidemic models and mathematical models of immunotherapy and tumor dynamics.

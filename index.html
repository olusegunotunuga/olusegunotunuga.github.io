Welcome

I am a faculty member in the Department of Mathematics at Augusta University. My research focuses on stochastic modeling, mathematical biology, epidemiology, and cancer dynamics.

My work develops mathematical frameworks for understanding complex biological systems including infectious diseases, tumor dynamics, and immunotherapy modeling.

This website contains information about my research, publications, and teaching.
